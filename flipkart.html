About Us

Deperpan Bastukar is an Authorised Dealer of the Electric Two-Wheeler Vehicle. We believe in offering an ecological 2-wheeler solution to our consumers, which above all else, gives our customers excellent value for money. Currently we are dealing Electric Two-Wheeler Vehicles of Deedav Motors and Evtric Motors. We endeavour to make the country greener with ‘Zero Pollution’ transportation through its wide range of Electric Vehicles, is supported by our Mission of “No Emission”. We are quality seekers and a quality conscious entity. All our products are closely inspected, rigorously tested by quality engineers. We are environmentally conscious, socially responsible and professionally ethical in our business operations.
